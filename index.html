One Piece: Battle of Legends

Battle Mode Only

Target Audience: Anime Fans, Casual Gamers

Development Team: Om Patel

Program Summary:

  • A simple 2D battle game where players select one One Piece
    protagonist to face off against a random AI opponent.
  • Each player performs a single attack to decide the winner.

Program Outline:

  • Players pick from Luffy, Zoro, or Sabo.
  • Fight random AI-controlled antagonists like Kaido, Big Mom, or Blackbeard.
  • Both players perform a single move to take away each other's HP and whoever's HP goes to zero loses.
